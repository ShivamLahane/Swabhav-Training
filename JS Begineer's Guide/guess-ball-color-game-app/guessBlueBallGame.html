Guess Blue Color Ball.

Game Rules :


  • There will be many number of balls on the screen, all will be grey in color initially, but their true colors reveal only when you click them
  • You have to guess the blue color ball which will be only one among whole group.
  • When you click right side ball of blue ball (which you have to guess) , the clicked ball will turn green.
  • As you click left side ball of the blue ball, it will turn red.
  • You will have only 5 chances to guess. Best of Luck!!!
